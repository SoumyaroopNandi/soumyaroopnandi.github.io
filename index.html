Welcome!

I'm a Computer Science PhD (USC). My work is on AI, Computer Vision, and Image Forensics — developing state-space and information-theoretic models for detecting generative image forgeries.

  • Ph.D. (USC): Context-Aware Semantic Forgery Detection for biomedical and natural images.
  • Interests: SSMs, VLMs, Diffusion Models, GANs, Representation Learning.

Research Interests

I’m broadly interested in understanding how complex, high-dimensional vision systems behave and how to model, analyze, and intervene in their internal causal pathways. I focus on generative models and representation learning.
